Your Brain on "Open Loops"

üß† What's an "Open Loop"?

Any commitment, task, or project that's incomplete and living in your head

Examples of Open Loops:

  • "I need to respond to Sarah's email"
  • "Must book that dentist appointment"
  • "The quarterly report is due soon"
  • "Should call Mom this weekend"
  • "Need to update my LinkedIn profile"
  • "That meeting follow-up from Tuesday"
  • "Fix the printer in the office"
  • "Plan team building event"

Why Open Loops Destroy Productivity

1. They Eat Mental RAM

Your brain constantly uses energy remembering and tracking incomplete items

2. They Create Instant Stress

Your psyche thinks you should be doing ALL of them at every moment

Reality Check: As soon as you have two things to do kept in mind, you've created instant and automatic stress and failure, because you can't do them both at once.
